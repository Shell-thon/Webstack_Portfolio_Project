About Me

I was born and raised in the city of Lagos At age 7, I became a computer user, I learnt Microsoft word and powerpoint during my primary school days. I became interested in the world of tech seeing how Softwares like Corel draw works, I learnt graphics designer at age 11 and started coding html, css and javascript at age 15

To cut the long story short, I am a Software Engineer and a Certified Cyber Security Specialist Who has passion in Information Security and Software Development. My goals are to build the world's most secure software and to help people with driven passion to become a Software Engineer and a Cyber Security Specialist. I specialize in the backend development of web applications using Python, C, and django as framework. I decided to go for backend development because I love to build things that are not visible to the eyes. Even after getting my certificate in Cyber Security, I still have a passion for backend development. I also have a good knowledge of frontend development using HTML, CSS, and Javascript and boostrap as framework.

I am a fast learner and I am always ready to learn new things. I've got skills in the following Areas of Information Security: OSINT, Vulnerability Assessment, Penetration Testing, Network Security, Cisco Networking. Areas of Programming: Python, C, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Django, Bootstrap. Areas of Graphics Design: Corel Draw, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator.

I am an undergraduate of Computer Engineering from the Yaba College of Technology.
